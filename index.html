About Us

We're builders of systemic solutions for the world's many problems. We're a family of initiaters, innovators, and inventors. Most of all we're inceptors of sustainable and scalable products and services for the world. #startupception

How we got started

We all met at the Next 36 National Selection Weekend, a highly coveted, super prestigious, incredibly reputable, respected, revered, and ultimately insanely selective entrepreneurial program for youth in Canada. Most of all though, it's one of the best ways to accelerate one's journey of becoming an entrepreneur during their prime as well as receiving the support of folks who want nothing more than to give back to Canada and elevate it on the economic world stage. This was a program that out of 1000+ applications, we the 3 cofounders were luckily selected as one of the 76 (reportedly; usually 72) finalists. It was our "relentless pursuit of opportunity without regard to resources currently controlled" is what drove us together.

We each brought different strengths to the table - product design, development, management, and business development. We each came together under shared passions in making a positive impact on society. We each came together under one banner of unrelentess fortitude to focus our livelihoods to the inception of bold startups. Our backgrounds in finance, health, and industrial processes, engineering, entrepreneurship, and ultimately systems & design thinking are what brought us together. Our combined interests in expanding the wealth and health of our society-at-large in a subversive way is how we got our start.
